Donations

Donate to help students and researchers explore the next generation of artificial intelligence.

It is an exciting time for research in artificial intelligence, with new advances increasingly shaping all our lives. However, attacking key challenges such as the reliability of AI requires a close collaboration of various research areas. Our vibrant and rapidly growing AI community at LMU is highly interdisciplinary and, therefore, perfectly suited to pave the way for the next generation of AI.

At present, a wide range of researchers at LMU from faculties in computer science, philosophy, medicine, anthropology, and many more are studying different aspects of AI with a significant impact on both public life and the sciences. Some exemplary highlights of AI research at LMU include work on combating online hate speech, helping management in businesses, improving journalism, helping to better target development aid, analyzing ancient Babylonian literature, studying the universe, or preventing illnesses.

Your contributions will help us facilitate this high-impact research across subjects. Here are some ways we plan to spend the money:

  • Prizes and stipends to support excellent students
  • Research equipment
  • Events to bring together researchers from diverse fields
  • Visits for exceptional researchers from and to other institutions

Unless you would like to donate to a particular project or have a special wish, which you can include in the Message field of the donation form, your donation will ensure that AI@LMU can always draw on funds to support AI research at LMU in the best possible way.

We thank you for your generosity!
