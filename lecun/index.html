Prof. Dr. Yann LeCun

Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences at New York University, Metas

Prof. Dr. Yann LeCun serves as the Silver Professor at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences at New York University and as Vice-President, Chief AI Scientist at Meta. LeCun's groundbreaking work includes the development of convolutional neural networks (CNNs) used in optical character recognition and computer vision, the creation of the DjVu image compression technology and the Lush programming language. He, along with Yoshua Bengio and Geoffrey Hinton, won the 2018 Turing Award for their work on deep learning, earning them the title "Godfathers of AI" and "Godfathers of Deep Learning."
